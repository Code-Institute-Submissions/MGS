Pathway to dynamic future

By visiting our website you are one step closer to a once in a lifetime experience! Studying abroad is a wonderful way to expand knowledge horizons.Making friends from all over the world and expanding your international network is just one of the advantages of studying overseas.You will be studying at one of the best educational institutes, as well as having the opportunity to travel and see all of the beautiful aspects of the destination of your choice.

By choosing to apply for an international study you are choosing to extend your possibilities on several levels: personal development, (social) network and it will increase your opportunities in the global job market. We at Mohar Global Services are here to guide you along the way. We want to offer you all the support you need. By choosing us as your support network we will make sure this experience is going to be valuable in every way!

How does this work

MGS will guide you from your first inquiry and answer all your questions until you decide a study course and country of your choice. Furthermore, MGS will help and support you in admissions, visa arrangements and housing abroad. We will comfort you throughout the entire procedure with best of our abilities.

MGS has a criteria of selecting students therefore all students must go through a screening procedure to evaluate the eligibility of the student. MGS assists student admission by following steps.

Step 1. MGS Telephonic interview with the student

Step 2. MGS Skype interview with the student

Step 3. Student and guardian signing the guarantee and assurance statement

Step 4. Continuation of the admission & visa process via MGS Netherlands

Please bear in mind that MGS does not promise any admission or letter of offer on behalf of any college or university. MGS also does not guarantee any visa success on behalf of any immigration authorities. We select our students carefully this is why we have introduce a screening procedure in order to make sure our admission and student visa success is 100%. MGS will charge you five hundred Euros in total for the full consultancy services. Half payment after the screening procedure and before starting the admission process and the other half after letter of admission is issued and before visa procedure.

Rise above yourself!

Studying abroad is a wonderful and lifetime and it helps to experience a new world and explore interesting cultures from across the globe. It also helps to learn several different languages as well as to travel the beautiful world. Making friends from all around the world and expanding international network is another advantage of studying at an international destination.

Studying overseas is an excellent decision because it provides proper self-esteem and helps to advance personal development and growth. By taking the opportunity to study in a different country one does not only get to see different places, but also takes an active part in everyday activities. This makes it easy to integrate into the society.

Furthermore, another possibility is to witness a new weather and climate and to take part in different sports. While studying overseas one can taste the delicious cuisine from different parts of the world and let others know about home cuisine. The whole experience of studying abroad is indeed gratifying and enriching because it offers international exposure, self-confidence and opens countless doors.
